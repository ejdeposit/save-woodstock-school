Portland Public Schools is presenting options (A and B) for changing the location of language immersion programs throughout Southeast Portland. This would significantly impact students at Woodstock. Option A ousts the Mandarin Immersion Program to Bridger, while simultaneously displacing many staff and students from the neighborhood program to make room for the the Creative Science School to occupy the building. Option B will relocate all students in the neighborhood program to several different nearby schools, leaving Woodstock as an all Mandarin Immersion Program school.
